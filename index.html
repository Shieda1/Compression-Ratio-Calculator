What is the compression ratio?
Compression ratio is the reatio between the volume of the cylinder and combustion chamber in an internal combustion engine at their maximum and minimum value.
How to calculate compression ratio?
To calculate compression ratio you should use the following formula:
CR = (Vd + Vc) / Vc
where:
CR = static compression ratio
Vd = displacement volume
Vc = clearance volume
Why use compression ratio?
The compression ratio is used in order to allow an engine to extract more mechanical energy from a given mass of air-fuel mixture due to it's higher thermal efficienty.
